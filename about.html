History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small developer meetup and has since evolved into one of the most anticipated technology conferences worldwide. Over the years, it has welcomed thousands of attendees and dozens of tech pioneers.

Mission

Our mission is to connect, educate, and inspire the global tech community by providing a platform for innovation, inclusion, and impactful conversations that shape the future of technology.
