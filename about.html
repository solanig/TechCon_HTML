Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. Over the years, it has grown into one of the premier conferences in the industry, drawing thousands of attendees from around the globe.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to inspire, educate, and connect technology leaders and innovators. Our mission is to foster collaboration, showcase groundbreaking innovations, and empower the next generation of tech pioneers.

Notable Past Speakers

Ada Lovelace

Recognized as the first computer programmer, Ada inspired our audience with a talk on the historical foundations of programming logic.

Alan Turing

A pioneer in computer science, Turing discussed the ethical implications of AI development and machine learning.

Grace Hopper

Grace Hopper captivated the audience with her insights into programming language development and her trailblazing journey in tech leadership.
